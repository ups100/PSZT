Each point should be written like this:
25 , 57 
where 25 is the X coordinate and 57 is the Y coordinate. Space beetween 
coordinates and semicolon is obligatory.

When all the segmenents point are written, there should be a "END" text.
Then the next segment may be written. Example:

45 , 76
46 , 435
45 , 123

END   /*End of segment*/
46 , 64 /*Beginning of the new Segment*/

There should be at least three points in each segment
File should also end with word END
